3. get movie by name path: get http://localhost:8080/movie , parameters:  movieName. if the movie is not exist return res = "this movie is not exist", else return the movie. V

5. write/update rate to movie - path: post http://localhost:8080/rate , parameters:  movieName, userName, rate. V
